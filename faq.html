The Study Lens is a student performance analytics and reporting system that collects test scores, attendance, and behavior data to give teachers, students, and parents a clear picture of academic progress.

The system aggregates marks from multiple assessments, converts them into percentages, and generates subject-wise and overall performance reports, including alerts for students below key thresholds like 50%, 75%, and 90%.

Teachers can view detailed analytics for all students in their classes, while students can only see their own marks, percentages, and reports through their login.
